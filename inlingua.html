How to use English phrasal verbs

Phrasal verbs are used just like verbs- you can use them anywhere they make sense!

Usually,the verb and preposition in a phrasal verb need to be said together,like in the phrase "fall down." in some cases, tough,you can separate the verb and the preposition by putting other words in between them.

For example,the phrase "turn off" can be used just like that,orit can be interrupted by stating what you're turning off. In other words,you can say"turn of the TV."but you can also say"turnthe TV off."Both are correct!
